To My Dearest,

i know we've been through a lot, and i respect what we had and where we are now. i'm not to ask something you're not ready to give. i just want you to know how i genuinely feel.

i remember what i promised you before— not as something you owe me, but as something i chose; that i would keep choosing you when things aren't easy, even when there's no certainty.

talking to you again reminded me of the parts i appreciated— not just as my girlfriend before, but as a person. i still care about you, and that hasn't changed.

i'm not saying this to rush you. i just want you to know that my intention stayed the same. i'm here because i want to be, not because of the past, but because of how i wholeheartedly feel.

i hope you know that i'm always here.
no pressure, no expectations, just honesty.

your love,
